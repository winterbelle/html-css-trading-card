  • Average Lifespan: 13-15 years

The Australian Shepherd (or "Aussie") is a highly intelligent, energetic, and versatile medium-sized herding dog, renowned as a loyal, "velcro" companion. Originally perfected in California, they possess a strong work drive, requiring intense daily exercise and mental stimulation, making them ideal for active owners, dog sports, or farm work.
